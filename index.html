NMTA (New Market Traders Association) is a dedicated and vibrant community organization that wholeheartedly supports local entrepreneurs. Our mission is to uplift and promote the growth of small businesses and local artisans in our region. Through various events, workshops, and initiatives, NMTA strives to create an inclusive platform that empowers entrepreneurs to thrive and contribute to the community's economic prosperity.
